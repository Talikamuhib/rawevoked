About this notebook

I'm learning neuroscience the hard way — in public, by doing. Every tutorial here is me figuring out a step of the analysis pipeline; every experiment is me pointing those tools at real data and seeing what the brain gives back.

Everything is open source and free. The write-ups, the notebooks, the mistakes. If it helps you learn too, that's the whole point. Fork it, break it, tell me where I'm wrong.
